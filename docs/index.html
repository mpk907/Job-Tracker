What this is

A daily-refreshed feed of digital, data, AI, software, product and adjacent technology roles across the global pharma value chain — Big Pharma, specialist pharma, AI-first biotech, digital-health scaleups & startups, CROs, MedTech, payers and pharma agencies.

How the data is collected

  • Direct ATS APIs (preferred): we hit the public job-board endpoints of Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, SmartRecruiters and Workday. ~60 companies covered directly.
  • Generic job-board APIs: Remotive, RemoteOK, Arbeitnow and The Muse — filtered by health/pharma keywords.
  • Watchlist: companies without a clean public API are surfaced as direct links to their careers pages.
  • Discovery: a daily Y Combinator scan flags newly-hiring health startups for review.

What counts as "digital"?

A title or department matching at least one of: digital, data, analytics, AI/ML/LLM, software/engineering, cloud, product manager, UX/UI, bioinformatics, computational, MLOps, digital health, digital therapeutic, telehealth, RWD/RWE, wearable, IoT, decentralized trial, MarTech, omnichannel.

Add a company

Edit scraper/companies.py and open a PR. Most digital- health scaleups use Greenhouse, Lever or Ashby — find their slug from the URL of their careers page (e.g. boards.greenhouse.io/<slug>) and add an entry.
